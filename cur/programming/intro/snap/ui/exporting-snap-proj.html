Congratulations! You have finished the very first lab of this course! To save your work, you have two options. We suggest either option 1 or option 2A to save your project.

    1. Exporting Your Snap! Project to Your Computer

    The point of exporting your project is so that you have a physical copy of it that you can keep on your desktop.

    1. Click on the file icon button on the upper left corner of the Snap! window.
    2. Select "Export Project."
    3. A new tab will open and you should see something that looks like this:
    4. Right click anywhere in the browser window, and select the Save As option.
    5. Name the file however you want. The file you are exporting will be in an .xml file format.
    6. Now save to your desktop or wherever you want on your computer.
