Think about how fast you could do this if you had the whole class to help. What would your strategy be? As partners, write up with a detailed description of your strategy; you teacher will have you share it with the rest of the class. Your fellow students should, after simply reading your description, be able to act it out! So make sure you provide enough clear detail.

After your fellow student teams have written their strategies, you will choose two of them and make a prediction about how long it would take. (Remember how long it took you working alone...)
