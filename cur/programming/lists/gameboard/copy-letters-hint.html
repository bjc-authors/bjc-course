Here is a hint for copy letters, we gave you a bit of a start and you and your partner should try to fill out the rest.

    You'll need:
    • a variable "index" to keep track of where you are in "word".
      • This will start at "min".
    • a variable "copy of letters" to hold the letters you've gathered so far.
      • This will start out blank
    • a loop that copies the letters at "index" into "copy of letters"
