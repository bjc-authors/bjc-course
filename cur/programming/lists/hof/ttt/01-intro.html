Introduction, and Asking Questions

    First things first, you're going to need the starter file.

    Now, try clicking the green flag to play the game against the AI! You'll notice that for an AI, it's a little... unintelligent. In the first part of this lab, you'll be upgrading the AI by building the block that will help choose the most optimal spot for the AI to select.

    Here's what we'd like out of the block:

    • Inputs: the current state of the game, and whether the computer is playing X or O.
    • Output: where the computer should move.

    If those don't sound super defined (current state, where to move), don't worry- we'll define them on the next page together.
